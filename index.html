About

My name is Anders Hoff, I am a generative artist and programmer. I am fascinated by how intricate and complex results you can get from a set of simple rules.

My works are often a result of trying to recreate some behaviour I have seen elsewhere. Either in nature, in previous works by others or in mathematical concepts.

Most of these algorithms are incredibly flexible, and I would love to hear from you if you have a project in mind! Or if you have a really large wall that seems kind of empty.
