Intro

Here are a few plots I've made using my Makeblock plotter. All plots were made using pigmented and light proof ink on high quality smooth cotton paper. Since they have been plotted using a mechanical plotter they all have their own little oddities and artefacts.

Please contact me at e-mail with questions, or if you want to buy a plot.

If you want to buy a plot please include the name of the plot (eg. "Plot A"), as well as your full name and address in the e-mail. I will then send you the payment details (Paypal). Note that I will not ship the plot until I have received they payment in full. All plots include standard shipping to anywhere in the world.

The plots will be signed and numbered. All plots below are unique, so they are numbered as "1/1".

I have more plots available, so contact me if you are missing something, and I might be able to make it available.
